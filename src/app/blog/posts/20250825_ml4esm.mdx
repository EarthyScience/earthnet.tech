We will be in Bonn for the [Machine Learning for the Earth System](https://cesoc.net/machine-learning-for-the-earth-system-programme/) workshop and the WeatherGenerator Hackathon. :notes:

Meet our team members Marieke, Markus, Sebastian and Vitus during the coffee breaks or at one of our contributions:

- Monday 25 Aug. 15:30 – 16:30 Marieke Wesselkamp presents [Towards high-resolution land surface temperature forecasting for early detection of extreme events](https://cesoc.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/all-abstracts.pdf#page=66)
- Tuesday 26 Aug. 11:00 – 12:00 Sebastian Hoffmann presents [Learning to Faithfully Compress Sentinel-2 Satellite Data using Vector-Quantized Autoencoders](https://cesoc.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/all-abstracts.pdf#page=77)
- Wednesday 27 Aug. 11:50 – 12:00 Vitus Benson presents [Neural networks in atmospheric transport and inverse modeling](https://cesoc.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/all-abstracts.pdf#page=52)
